About Me

Beyond my technical responsibilities, I am motivated by curiosity and the constant desire to understand how geological systems work. Fieldwork is an essential part of who I am as a geologist — I enjoy travelling, working in new environments, and interpreting geological processes directly from observation and evidence.

I value interacting with people from different cultures and learning from diverse perspectives, as these experiences broaden my professional and personal understanding of the world. I enjoy walking in natural landscapes and diving, activities that allow me to connect with the environment in meaningful, reflective ways. For me, geology represents continuous exploration, growth, and learning.
