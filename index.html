Professional Profile

With 20 years of experience in geology, I have worked in a variety of roles, including field and office work. I have also been fortunate to work in different countries (Colombia, China, and Canada), gaining knowledge of their geology and working methodologies.

I specialize in integrating mapping, geochemistry, structural geology, and spatial data analysis to support target definition in complex geological environments. My expertise includes regional geochemical mapping, drill core logging, geochemical data interpretation and QA/QC, and drill target definition in structurally controlled systems. I am proficient in ArcGIS, MapInfo, and IoGAS, with experience in database management, 3D data integration, and multidisciplinary team leadership.

My approach is data-driven and field-grounded, focused on reducing geological uncertainty through rigorous analysis and integrated interpretation.
