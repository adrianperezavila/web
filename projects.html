Selected Projects

Throughout my career, I have contributed to mineral exploration programs across multiple stages — from regional geochemical reconnaissance to advanced target definition, data validation, and technical reporting. The following projects highlight representative leadership roles, technical integration, and quality assurance responsibilities.

Colombian Geological Survey (2011–Present)
Contributor to national mineral prospectivity programs through systematic stream sediment, soil, and water geochemistry. Work includes QA/QC, statistical analysis, anomaly delineation, and integrated interpretation supporting resource potential assessments.

Geochemical Atlases of Colombia (2016–2022)
Directed the development and technical integration of four national-scale geochemical atlases for the Colombian Geological Survey, synthesizing multi-element datasets under strict QA/QC protocols to ensure analytical reliability and national-scale mineral resource assessment consistency.

Colombia–China Low-Density Geochemical Mapping
Led a bilateral cooperation program between Colombia and China focused on regional geochemical baseline mapping, overseeing field operations, analytical QA/QC procedures, laboratory coordination, data validation workflows, and inter-institutional technical reporting.

Uranium Exploration – Central Colombia
Managed uranium-focused exploration campaigns integrating geochemical and geophysical datasets, supervising drilling operations, implementing QA/QC standards for sampling and analytical programs, and contributing to NI 43-101 compliant technical documentation.

Shebandowan Greenstone Belt – Canada (MSc Research)
Conducted structural and geochemical investigations on Archean shear zones controlling gold mineralization, applying rigorous analytical validation and data quality control procedures, contributing to peer-reviewed research published in the Journal of Structural Geology (2025).

Ring of Fire Metals – Nickel Exploration (Wyloo)
Compiled, digitized, validated, and integrated historical geochemical, geophysical, and drilling datasets under structured QA/QC review to support nickel exploration targeting and prospectivity modeling in Northern Ontario.

Geological Mapping – Official Cartography
Author of official 1:100,000-scale geological maps integrating field mapping, structural interpretation, petrography, and geospatial analysis. These maps contribute to Colombia’s national geological framework.
